Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register: email address, encrypted password. When downloading: IP address (for daily download limits). We do not collect names, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

2. How We Use Information

Email: account authentication, password reset, subscription notifications. IP address: guest daily download limit (3/day), automatically cleared after 24 hours.

3. Data Storage

User data is stored in Turso Cloud (AWS Tokyo region). All data transmitted via encrypted connections (HTTPS). Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (one-way, irreversible).

4. Third-Party Services

Payment processing: Lemon Squeezy / Creem (handles payment details; we never access your credit card number). Email delivery: Resend. Video parsing: TikHub API (only video URLs are transmitted).

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time in Settings, which permanently removes all personal data. Contact support@orangedl.com to request a data export. Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data.

6. Contact

Privacy questions: support@orangedl.com
